Rules:

- No opinion words, no editorializing, no "shocking," "slams," "blasts," etc.

Articles:
Article 1: Rs 62,500 crore push for mobile manufacturing Will it make smartphones cheaper. The Union Cabinet approved the Mobile Phone Manufacturing Scheme worth Rs 62,500 crore to boost domestic production, exports and local value addition.

A separate Rs 1,27,500 crore allocation was made for Semicon 2.0 to advance local chip design and semiconductor sovereignty. Tamil Nadu, which accounts for 38 to 40 per cent of India's smartphone shipments, is expected to benefit significantly from the scheme.
